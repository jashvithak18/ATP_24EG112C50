BANGLORE

Bangalore (Bengaluru) is famous as India's "Silicon Valley" for its IT industry, but also for its lush parks like Lalbagh & Cubbon Park, historical sites like Bangalore Palace & Tipu Sultan's Palace, vibrant nightlife with numerous breweries, and cultural spots like the ISKCON Temple, alongside attractions such as Bannerghatta National Park and the majestic Vidhana Soudha.
