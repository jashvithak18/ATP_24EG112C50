CHENNAI

Chennai is famous as India's cultural hub for Carnatic music, Bharatanatyam dance, and Kollywood (Tamil film industry), a major IT/auto manufacturing center dubbed "Detroit of Asia," and home to the scenic Marina Beach, historic temples, and renowned filter coffee and cuisine. It's a blend of tradition and modernity, known for its vibrant festivals, strong educational institutions like IIT Madras, and its significant economic role as a key port and industrial city.
